## Permissions Explained

The extension requests minimal permissions:

### `contextMenus`
- **Purpose:** Add right-click menu items for PDF links
- **Data Access:** Only URLs of PDF files you right-click on
- **Usage:** To open LocalPDF tools with the correct URL

### `notifications`
- **Purpose:** Show welcome notification on first install
- **Data Access:** None
- **Usage:** One-time notification when extension is installed

### `storage`
- **Purpose:** Save your language preference
- **Data Access:** Only your chosen language setting (e.g., "en", "ru")
- **Usage:** Remember your preferred language across sessions

### `host_permissions` (localpdf.online)
- **Purpose:** Open LocalPDF website in new tabs
- **Data Access:** None
- **Usage:** Navigate to the correct tool page when you click a tool

## Third-Party Services

The extension opens https://localpdf.online in your browser. LocalPDF's website privacy policy applies when you use the website:
- All PDF processing happens in your browser
- No files are uploaded to servers
- See https://localpdf.online for full website privacy policy
